Welcome to The Lakeside

Welcome to Howard's Lakeside General Store. Here is a brief history about our store and the lake.

In the autumn of 1974, the C. J. Brown Reservoir was built principally for flood control. The Lake is part of the Buck Creek State Park, it is comprised of 2,120-acre lake with over 12 miles of shoreline is located approximately 7 miles above the Mad River.

About us

We are a small general store beside the lake that sell different bait and fish. Additionally, we sell caught and cooked. We are primarily a fishing store that cooks and sells fish. We also have a reel restore service to fix or respool a fishing rod.
